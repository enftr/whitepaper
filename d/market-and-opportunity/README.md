# Market & Opportunity

### The Global Metaverse Market

The metaverse is an amalgamation of the digital world in which physical, augmented and virtual realities are converged. The metaverse is like an anastomosis between the physical and digital world "_**Phygital**_". It provides lucrative opportunities for a variety of companies in various markets and sectors. \
\
The Global Market revenue crossed $58.5B in 2021 and it is expected to advance at a CAGR of 43.7% during 2021-2030 to reach $1525.7B by 2030. This is ascribed to the surging demand of metaverse to purchase digital assets using cryptocurrencies.
